Mexican Rice

A Dish to Bring the Table and Food Together

Rice is one of the most common foods we use in our daily live to substain and create more balanced meals. As it is a grain and full of carbohydrates, it helps fill the body with short term energy and exapnds in size to make people feel more full when eating. It is also a great way to bring together people with its cheap production cost as well as versatility when pairing with other foods

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Long-grain Rice
  • 1 Cup Chicken Stock
  • 1/2 Cup Tomato Juice
  • 1/2 Cup Water
  • Tablespoon (Tbs) Cumin Seasoning
  • Tablespoon (Tbs) Onion Powder
  • Vegetable or Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Wash a cup of rice in a strain-able container or bowl to wash off starch residue
  2. In your pan for steaming rice, add a small amount of oil to the pan and then your washed rice
  3. let raw rice brown in the oil then add wet ingredients to the pan
  4. add spices into the pan next and stir adequately
  5. let it boil before lowering heat to a low setting and covering pan with a lid
  6. Let the pan simmer for 20 minutes with occasional stirring to prevent sticking to the bottom
  7. If there is no liquid viewable in pan, turn off heat, stir, and then serve!
