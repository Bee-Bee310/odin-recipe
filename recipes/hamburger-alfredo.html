Hamburger Alfredo

Creamy and Simple

Looking for a simple pasta dish that can feed one or the entire family? This is the perfect one that's beginner friendly, has easy instructions, a savory yet creamy taste, and teaches simple syncopation in the kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/8 tsp Ground Pepper
  • 1/8 tsp Oregano Leafs
  • 1/2 Pound of Ground Beef
  • 1 Jar of Classico Alfredo
  • Rotini Noodles

Steps:

  1. Fill a pot with water, a dash of salt, and place over heat to bring to a boil
  2. Once boiling, add Rotini noodles to the pot and reduce to a low heat to make a steady boil
  3. While noodles are boiling, get out a small pan for the ground beef
  4. Place ground beef in pan and turn on heat. While waiting for it to warm, add pepper; oregano; and salt to the top of ground beef
  5. Break up ground beef with spatula and mix in pan while it continues to cook until it completes browns
  6. Drain fat off of the hamburger meet and place on layered paper towel after it finishes cooking so you can press out the fat from the beef
  7. Around this time your pasta should be getting close to a nice softness, test one to see if it's consistency is to your liking
  8. Strain your noodles into a colander and leave those to the side
  9. In the pot you used to make the noodles, add the alfredo into the pot and let it heat until you notice boiling
  10. After pressing out the fat from you ground beef, add it to the alfredo along with your noodles
  11. Stir and Serve
